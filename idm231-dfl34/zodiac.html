Mario

Mario loves to be number one, showing up on almost every game in his franchise. He is an ambitious protagonist, who is always out to save the day. His abilities include using fireballs.

Luigi

Luigi loves for things to be calm and balanced, but his mansion wrecks havoc on his life. He is shy and often pushed to the side for characters like Mario to get attention. His abilities include vacuuming ghosts.

Wario

Wario is a greedy, greedy man. I heard he once took candy from a baby, and sold it back for a profit. Wario's motivated by money, like most people. His abilities include smelling bad.

Peach

Peach has come to be known as a damsel in distress, but she is so much more. She is kind, athletic, and of course royalty. She even saves Mario in a few games of her own. Her abilities include using an umbrella.

Waluigi

Waluigi loves to prank people. A fan favorite amongst the crowd, but often left out of games. He's known to be a real wild card, from everything from racing to tennis. His abilities include more than we could ever know.

Goomba

Goomba is loved by all. Who could hate him? Goomba's love to hang out with each other and can often be found stacking on top of each other to fight against Mario's crew. Goomba has been known to work for Bowser, but switches sides depending on how he is feeling. His abilities include being a glass canon.

Yoshi

Yoshi has been known to be a fun and cooperative character! He is always willing to help others. He is known to steal food from the other characters when they aren't looking. His abilities include being able to run on air.

Donkey Kong

Donkey Kong is tenacious and loyal. No matter how many times his bananas get taken, he doesn't switch to another fruit. He goes after them, gets them back, and stomps all over anyone that gets in his face along the way. His abilities include being able to eat a lot of bananas.

Toad

Toad can sometimes be timid and cowardly. Despite his fears, he has become a successful explorer and treasure tracker. His abilities include being able to conquer his fears.

Rosalina

Rosalina is shown to be a wise, mature, motherly and kind woman, who has made protecting the cosmos her mission in life. She is a loving mother figure to the Lumas, caring for her children diligently. Her abilities include manipulating the stars.

Daisy

Daisy is competitive and lively, but is also confident and kind. She's a jack of all trades in sports. Her abilities include being able to fight with a golf club.

Bowser

Bowser is angry, immature, and selfish. Bowser is a short-tempered turtle, who only wants to wreak havoc on the Mushroom Kingdom in the pursuit of power. Conceited and possessive, Bowser will stop at nothing to crush his foes. His abilities include being a fire breathing turtle.
